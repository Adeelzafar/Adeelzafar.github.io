About

I am a postdoctoral researcher working on machine learning systems for industrial data analysis. My research interests focus on Natural Language Processing (NLP), Large Language Models (LLMs), and AI Security. My work explores building reliable and secure AI systems including named entity recognition, policy enforcement systems, adversarial evaluation of language models, and applied machine learning solutions for industrial and enterprise environments.

I am particularly interested in bridging academic research with real-world deployments of AI systems in industry.

Research Areas

  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • AI Security and Red Teaming
  • Named Entity Recognition
  • Industrial Machine Learning
  • Multilingual AI Systems
