About Me

I am a Machine Learning researcher currently working as a Postdoctoral Researcher at Halmstad University, Sweden. My research focuses on machine learning, deep learning, generative AI, and industrial AI applications.

I received my PhD in Computer Science from National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences (FAST-NUCES). My research expertise includes NLP, recommendation systems, procedural content generation, and predictive modeling.
