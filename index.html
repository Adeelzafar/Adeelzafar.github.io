About

I am a postdoctoral researcher working in machine learning and artificial intelligence. My research focuses on natural language processing, large language models, privacy-preserving AI, and real-world deployment of machine learning systems. My work spans areas such as named entity recognition, multilingual NLP, AI safety, and applied machine learning systems.
